Grassroots Drift and Touge

After 1.5 years of rigorous research, testing, and development, I'm proud to present the Grassroots Drift and Touge car pack. This pack is the culmination of countless hours spent fine-tuning the physics, suspension, and tire dynamics to deliver an authentic drifting experience in Assetto Corsa. Influenced by car packs such as WDTS, Deathwish Garage, and Excite, this pack goes beyond the limitations of traditional Assetto Corsa physics, introducing enhanced realism with extended tire wear, cold tire dynamics, and variable grip settings. The goal was to create a training tool for sim drifters and real-world drivers alike, offering an experience that pushes you to improve reaction times, handle understeer, and manage the car in challenging scenarios. Each car has been carefully designed to bring out unique handling characteristics, with adjustable settings for the dedicated tuner. This isn't just another drift pack; it's a labor of love aimed at the true enthusiasts who want to push their skills to the limit. I hope you enjoy driving these cars as much as I enjoyed creating them, and I look forward to seeing the community grow with this new addition.
